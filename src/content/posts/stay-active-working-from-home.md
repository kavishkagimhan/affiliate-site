## Solution 2: Structured Movement Breaks

### The Science-Backed Approach

Research shows **breaking up sitting every 30-60 minutes** dramatically reduces health risks - almost as effectively as continuous exercise.

### The Every-Hour Method

**Set a timer for every hour. Do 3-5 minutes of:**

**Option 1: Quick Walk**
- Walk around house/apartment
- Up/down stairs if available
- Target: 300-500 steps
- **Time: 3-4 minutes**

**Option 2: Simple Exercises**
- 20 squats
- 20 jumping jacks
- 30-second plank
- 10 push-ups
- **Time: 3-5 minutes**

**Option 3: Stretch Routine**
- Neck rolls (30 seconds)
- Shoulder rolls (30 seconds)
- Torso twists (1 minute)
- Hip flexor stretch (1 minute each side)
- Hamstring stretch (1 minute)
- **Time: 5 minutes**

**Results over 8-hour workday:**
- 8 movement breaks

- 2,400-4,000 extra steps
- Improved energy and focus

**Work structure:**
- 25 minutes focused work (sitting)

**Activity break ideas:**
- Quick walk
- Bodyweight exercises
- Stretching

**Why it works:**

- Prevents decision fatigue
- Builds consistent habit
- Improves productivity AND health
