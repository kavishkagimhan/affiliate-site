### Optimal Brewing Tips

To get the best results:

1. **Use Fresh Beans**: Grind just before brewing when possible
2. **Correct Grind Size**: Medium grind works best for drip coffee makers
3. **Proper Ratios**: Use 1-2 tablespoons of coffee per 6 ounces of water
4. **Clean Regularly**: Descale monthly for best performance
5. **Fresh Water**: Use filtered water for better taste

Proper maintenance ensures optimal performance:

- **Daily Cleaning**: Rinse carafe and filter basket after each use
