## Why Choose a Mechanical Keyboard?

Mechanical keyboards offer several advantages over standard membrane keyboards:

- **Better Typing Feel**: Tactile feedback and responsive keys
- **Durability**: Individual mechanical switches last much longer
- **Customization**: Multiple switch types, keycaps, and layouts
- **Performance**: Faster actuation for gaming and typing
- **Satisfaction**: More enjoyable typing experience

## Understanding Switch Types

Mechanical keyboards use different switch types, each with unique characteristics:

### Linear Switches
- Smooth keystroke with no tactile bump
- Quiet operation
- Popular for gaming
- Example: Red switches

### Tactile Switches
- Bump felt during keystroke
- Good balance of feel and noise
- Popular for typing
- Example: Brown switches

### Clicky Switches
- Audible click sound
- Very tactile feedback
- Classic mechanical feel
- Example: Blue switches

The keyboard we're reviewing comes with multiple switch options, allowing you to choose what feels best for your needs.
