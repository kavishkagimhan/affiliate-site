## Common Myths Debunked

### Myth 1: "Walking Pads Are Always Better Quality"

**Reality:** While WalkingPad brand is generally good quality, many generic under desk treadmills match or exceed their quality‚Äîespecially premium brands like Lifespan. Quality depends more on specific model and price point than terminology.

### Myth 2: "Under Desk Treadmills Have Handlebars, Walking Pads Don't"

**Reality:** This isn't a defining difference. Many under desk treadmills are handlebar-free, and some come with removable handlebars. It's a design choice, not a category distinction.

### Myth 3: "Walking Pads Are More Compact"

**Reality:** While WalkingPad brand emphasizes compact design, many generic under desk treadmills fold just as thin (5-6"). Compactness is about specific model design, not category.

### Myth 4: "You Can't Run on a Walking Pad"

**Reality:** Some WalkingPad models (like R series) support speeds up to 10 km/h, suitable for light jogging. The name doesn't limit functionality.

### Myth 5: "Walking Pads Are Always Quieter"

**Reality:** Noise level depends on motor quality, not product name. Many generic brands with brushless motors are equally quiet or quieter than WalkingPad models.
